About Model Muse

Zimbabwe's Premier Modeling Agency

Founded in 2010, Model Muse International has established itself as Zimbabwe's leading modeling agency, representing diverse talent for the global fashion industry.

We specialize in discovering, developing, and promoting exceptional models for fashion shows, commercial campaigns, and editorial work worldwide.

Our agency provides comprehensive model management services, including professional development, portfolio building, and international placement.
